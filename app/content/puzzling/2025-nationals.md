You can read more [about the competition](https://www.usajigsaw.org/2025-nationals).

## Individual

Individual competitions are held on 500pc unreleased Ravensburger puzzles, and competitors have an hour and a half to either complete or finish as much of the puzzle as possible.

I competed in the Individual C Prelimiary round, the first round of competition that determines your placement in finals. I completed 299 peices of the puzzle and ranked 146th, which did not qualify me for finals (the top 65 moved on). See the [results on worldjigsaw.org](https://worldjigsawpuzzle.org/usajpa/nationals2025/individual/C).

## Pairs

Pairs competitions are held on 500pc unreleased Ravensburger puzzles, and competitors have an hour and 15 minutes to either complete or finish as much of the puzzle as possible.

I competed with Chelsea Jacobs, the organizer of [DC Puzzle Competition](https://www.dcpuzzlecompetition.com/), in Pairs A Prelimiary round. We completed the puzzle in 1:01:07 and ranked 128th, which did not qualify us for finals (the top 101 moved on). See the [results on worldjigsaw.org](https://worldjigsawpuzzle.org/usajpa/nationals2025/pairs/A).
